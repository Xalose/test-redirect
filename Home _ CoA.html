    How it works

    You:

    • You can send in a question anonymously in the forum.  OR you can send it in the chat if you don't mind being public when we are online.

    • Please only send in questions relating to academics, we, unfortunately, aren't qualified to provide therapy :( However, we can give you virtual hugs <3

    • Please be civilized in the chat

    Us:

    • We will answer you within the day the question is asked (after 19:30, we'll answer the next morning) on the advice page.

    • We will post articles on helpful tips & advice here

    • If we can't answer you, we will find another source of information that can!
